What did you learn about the importance of checking your sources for credibility?

There is a substantial amount of accessible fake news and sometimes it is not obvious whether the article you are reading is real or fake. Team Chicago's goal is to expose you to how easy it is to come across fake news and potentially spread information that is false. In a world where there is so much going on, we all want to make sure we are benefiting society rather than hindering it. You can contribute to that by using credible sources.
